It is an intense process that places importance on the quality of therapeutic relationship between the client and the counsellor, to be built on empathic understanding, respect, genuineness, non-judgmental atmosphere and positive regard. During the sessions, the counsellor creates a safe space for the client and utilizes the knowledge of her theoretical orientation and various counselling tools and techniques. The client and the counsellor understand the problem better to collaboratively set goals for therapy. The client then explores, evaluates, clarifies and understands his/ her patterns of thoughts, feelings and behaviors and uses these insights and in-built strengths to make the desired change. In all cases, counselling should result in free and responsible behavior on the part of the client, accompanied by more insight into him or herself and ability to understand and better manage negative emotions. The tools and techniques offered by the counsellor are also utilized by the client for the same.
